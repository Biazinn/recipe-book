#1 Mousse

  • Time: 20min
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 3
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Semi-sweet chocolate 200g
Brazilian Milk cream 1 can

Step 1: Mix the egg yolks until they double in volume.

Step 2: Add the sugar and keep mixing.

Step 3: Melt the chocolate and add the mixed yolks.

Step 4: Add the Brazilian milk cream, while still mixing.

Step 5: Finally, add the egg whites (well mixed), mixing quickly.

Step 6: Refrigerate and serve, enjoying the best and easiest mousse to make!

#2 Bread

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 3
Butter 3 tablespoons
Warm milk 1 and a quarter cup
Wheat flour 7 cups
Sugar 5 tablespoons
Biological Yeast Fleischmann 13g
Salt 1 teaspoon
Yolk to "brush" 1

Step 1: In the blender, add the eggs, sugar, Biological Yeast, butter, salt and warm milk.

Step 2: Mix for 2 minutes. The milk cannot be too hot, otherwise it will kill the yeast bacteria.

Step 3: Transfer the contents to a bowl and add a bit of wheat flour, stirring with a spoon.

Step 4: Gradually add the wheat and stir until the dough becomes heavy.

Step 5: Sprinkle wheat on a surface and place the dough. Sprinkle wheat on the dough as well and mix with your hands until you have a nice, smooth dough. There is no need to knead it, it is an easy dough to work with.

Step 6: Make a ball with the dough and let it rest in a covered container for 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

Step 7: Once the dough has risen, divide the bread dough into two parts, mix a little more and roll out one of them with a rolling pin.

Step 8: Roll the dough so that it has nice edges and do the same with the other half.

Step 9: Place both parts in a greased pan, cover with plastic wrap and let rest for another hour.

Step 10: The bread dough will have risen well. Brush it with an egg yolk and bake in a preheated oven at 200º C for 25 minutes and it's ready.

Step 11: Just enjoy this fluffy homemade bread. It's delicious with butter.

#3 Cake

  • Time: 40min
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 2 cups
Margarine 4 tablespoons
Milk 1 and a half teaspoon
Wheat flour 3 cups
Egg 3
Baking Powder 1 full tablespoon

Step 1: Beat the egg whites until stiff and set aside.

Step 2: Mix the egg yolks, margarine and sugar until you obtain a homogeneous dough.

Step 3: Add the milk and wheat flour little by little, without stop mixing.

Step 4: Finally, add the beaten egg whites and the baking powder.

Step 6: Pour the batter into a large greased and floured ring-shaped pan.

Step 7: Bake in a preheated oven at 180 °C for approximately 40 minutes or until the cake is clean when pierced with a fork.
